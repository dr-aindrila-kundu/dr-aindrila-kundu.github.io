About

A Surgeon Who Listens.
A Doctor Who Cares.

Dr. Aindrila Kundu is a dedicated Obstetrician, Gynaecologist, and Laparoscopic Specialist practising in Kolkata. With over 5 years of experience, she has delivered 700+ babies and managed 100+ high-risk pregnancies with precision and heart.

Her calling began during internship, where she discovered a profound love for women’s health. That passion has only deepened, driving her to pursue advanced qualifications and stay current with evidence-based practice. She approaches every patient as a whole person — not just a case file. To see a happy, healthy mother and baby, she says, makes every exhausting moment profoundly worthwhile.
